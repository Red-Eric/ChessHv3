// TOGGLES
AUTO MOVE OFF Engine automatically plays the best move after the configured delay. Disable to only receive move suggestions without the engine playing on your behalf.
ACCURACY + ELO OFF Displays a live accuracy percentage and estimated ELO rating based on the quality of moves played during the current game session.
MOVE CLASSIFICATION OFF Labels each move as Brilliant, Best, Excellent, Good, Inaccuracy, Mistake, or Blunder based on how it compares to the engine's top evaluation line.
WINNING MOVES ONLY OFF Filters displayed arrows to only show moves that maintain or improve a winning advantage. No arrows will appear in equal or losing positions.
EVAL BAR OFF Shows a vertical evaluation bar beside the board indicating which side holds the advantage. The white section grows as the position becomes more favorable for white.
HIDE ARROWS OFF Hides all move arrows on the board. Only the eval bar and statistics remain visible. Ideal for a clean stream overlay or more discreet use during a game.
